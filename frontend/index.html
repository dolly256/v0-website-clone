Entrepreneur's Guide

How to Become an Entrepreneur

Learn the essential skills and strategies to start and grow your business successfully. Master finance and marketing fundamentals.

Budgeting Essentials

  • Calculate startup costs and initial investment
  • Track daily expenses and income
  • Set aside emergency funds (3-6 months)
  • Plan for taxes and legal fees
  • Review and adjust budget monthly

Funding Options

  • Government schemes and subsidies
  • Bank loans with low interest rates
  • Microfinance institutions
  • Self-help group (SHG) loans
  • Angel investors and crowdfunding

Bookkeeping Basics

  • Maintain daily sales and expense records
  • Separate personal and business finances
  • Use digital tools for accounting
  • Understand profit margins and pricing
  • Plan for business growth and expansion
